### Quick verdict
Raising your laptop screen to eye level is the single most effective change to reduce neck strain. These stands are stable, portable, and easy to pair with a Bluetooth keyboard.

## Why this matters
Looking down at a laptop screen for hours puts constant stress on your neck muscles. A stand that elevates the screen to eye level keeps your spine in alignment.

## What to look for
- Height range
- Portability
- Ventilation for heat
- Stability with external keyboard

## How to choose
Pair with an external keyboard and mouse. If you move around, buy a foldable model.

## Setup tips
Position the top of the screen at eye level and the keyboard at comfortable typing height. Use a laptop riser with good airflow for thermal management.

### Final thoughts
An ergonomic laptop stand is an inexpensive tweak that yields immediate comfort improvements.
